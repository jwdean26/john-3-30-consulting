About John 3:30 Consulting, LLC

John 3:30 Consulting, LLC specializes in helping individuals with physical or cognitive challenges use their computers, tablets, and smartphones with confidence.

The focus is to research, test, recommend, implement, and support the assistive technology hardware, software, and services that best meet each person’s unique needs.

Whether someone is navigating vision loss, hearing differences, mobility limitations, speech challenges, or cognitive barriers, the goal is to make technology easier, more comfortable, and more accessible.

Services

Accommodation Recommendations

Personalized accommodation recommendations tailored to each individual’s needs, including guidance on choosing the right assistive technology tools and solutions.

Accessibility Settings Assistance

Help adjusting accessibility settings on:

  • Windows
  • macOS
  • iPadOS
  • iOS
  • Android

Support for learning and using built‑in accessibility apps across PCs, tablets, and smartphones.

Assistive Technology Setup & Training

Installation and configuration of assistive technology software, along with patient, practical training to help users feel comfortable and confident.

Ergonomic & Hardware Recommendations

Assistance selecting the most appropriate ergonomic keyboard and/or mouse, and help choosing the right headset for comfort, clarity, and accessibility.

Virtual Support

Remote support for hardware, software, and accessibility troubleshooting, so users can get help from wherever they are.
